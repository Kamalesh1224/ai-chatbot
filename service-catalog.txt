Northstar Labs offers website redesigns, landing page builds, knowledge-base chatbots, CRM-connected lead capture, onboarding content systems, and lightweight analytics dashboards. Projects are designed to improve response time, reduce repetitive support work, and make high-intent visitors easier to convert.

The chatbot service includes a branded chat interface, retrieval from text or PDF knowledge files, visible source snippets, and conversation memory so short follow-up questions still make sense. Example knowledge sources include onboarding guides, pricing notes, support policies, implementation checklists, and internal FAQ documents.

For companies that already have content in PDFs, text files, Markdown, or JSON exports, the chatbot can index that material and answer from the most relevant sections. If a document does not contain a reliable answer, the preferred behavior is to say that clearly and route the visitor to a person.

Implementation can also include custom prompts, lead capture forms, escalation rules, and analytics around unanswered questions so the knowledge base gets better over time.
