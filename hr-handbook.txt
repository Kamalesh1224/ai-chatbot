Northstar Labs uses a remote-first operating model with core collaboration hours from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Pacific. Outside those shared hours, employees are expected to keep calendars current and communicate handoffs clearly across time zones.

Full-time employees receive paid time off, local holidays, and a flexible sick leave policy. Vacation requests should be submitted in advance through the team planning workflow, and extended leave should be coordinated with the employee's manager and people operations contact.

New hires go through a structured onboarding plan that covers company context, team introductions, security basics, tooling access, and role-specific goals for the first thirty days. Managers are responsible for making sure each new employee has a documented ramp plan before the start date.

People operations also manages recruiting coordination, interview scheduling, policy questions, and benefits guidance. Questions about compensation changes, personal employee matters, or confidential cases should always be escalated to a human instead of answered speculatively by the chatbot.

When HR material is added to the knowledge base, it should stay clearly separated from product and support documentation. Topic-aware retrieval is important because a short follow-up such as "what about onboarding?" could mean employee onboarding in an HR context or customer onboarding in a product context.
